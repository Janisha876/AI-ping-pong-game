AI ping-pong game











Developed by whiteHat jr team

Instructions

  1. First keep your laptop screen straight
  2. Move yourselves three-four feet away from the laptop
  3. Move your right wrist upwards and downwards,a red dot will appear on your right wrist
  4. Now,press play button and again move away three-four feet
  5. the red paddle is your paddle which will move as per your right wrist
